Moon Rover 2035

Realistic Lunar Driving Simulator, made by Unity

Collaborative Project
Programmer, UI Designer, 3D Artist
Type of the project: Educational Game
Keywords: Serious game, Driving simulation, Motion controls, Realistic rendering
Time: 2021

This is a serious game about driving a lunar rover. The main purpose is to create an immersive science experience. The player takes on the role of a lunar geology researcher, who is on a mission to drive a lunar rover to explore the lava caves in a moon crater and help accelerate the scientific progress of the lunar exploration project.
We have created a lunar environment as real as possible. Players will be familiar with the special gravity of the Moon, learn about lunar soil, lunar crater lava caves and other aerospace knowledge while driving the lunar rover, and possibly develop an interest about aerospace, thus achieving the purpose of education and fun.

Overview

Background

In the future, Chinese spaceflight has deployed science bases on the Moon, sending researchers to conduct scientific exploration of lunar craters. You, as a lunar geology researcher, are currently on a mission to drive a manned lunar rover to take photos and conduct mineral sampling in the ancient lava caves of lunar craters to help accelerate the scientific research progress of the lunar exploration project.
